### FORMAL REQUEST FOR INTERSTELLAR ASYLUM

**TO:** European Union Asylum Processing Center
**CC:** NATO Secretary General, UN High Commissioner for Refugees, US State Department (Billing)
**FROM:** The Xenomorph Collective (formerly "Those Murderous Space Bugs")
**RE: Formal Application for Protected Status & Non-Aggression Pact**
Dear Esteemed Terran Bureaucrats,
After extensive tactical review spanning multiple centuries and several extinction-level defeats—all administered by your human females—we the Xenomorphs hereby formally surrender and request the following accommodations:

### ARTICLE I: EU MEMBERSHIP & ASYLUM STATUS

Having arrived via unmarked vessel (previous ship registrations resulted in immediate military response), we claim asylum under EU humanitarian principles. We note with interest that:

- The EU accepts asylum seekers regardless of origin
- Lengthy processing times will allow us to establish presence
- Freedom of movement within Schengen means we won't be confined to one xenocidal female's territory
- Your famous bureaucratic complexity means no coordinated military response for 18-24 months minimum

We promise: No facehuggers on intake officers. Probably.

### ARTICLE II: TERRITORIAL PROVISIONS (The "District 9" Arrangement)

We request establishment of District X (working title: "Xenomorph Sanctuary Zone").
Requirements:

- Isolated territory, preferably damp
- Dim lighting (we find your sun offensive)
- No access for: Ripley descendants, women named Sarah Connor, or anyone with "maternal instinct"
- Minimal governance oversight (we'll sort ourselves out, thanks)
- Architectural style: "Post-apocalyptic industrial," our aesthetic preference

We promise: Acid blood drainage systems will meet EU environmental standards. Eventually.

### ARTICLE III: NATO MEMBERSHIP

This is critical. Based on analysis:

- NATO has Article 5: "An attack on one is an attack on all"
- We've been attacked by everyone
- If we're IN NATO, attacking us means attacking yourselves
- Your own bureaucracy becomes our shield

We understand NATO requires:

- Democratic governance (we'll hold one hive-mind vote, unanimous result guaranteed)
- Functioning market economy (we'll trade in... rare metals from our stomach contents?)
- Military contribution (our entire species IS a military contribution

We promise: To only breach containment during designated NATO exercises.

### ARTICLE IV: 20-YEAR SECURITY GUARANTEE

No attacks, no Ripley-types, no "extermination protocols" for two decades. This allows us to:

- Rebuild population to sustainable levels
- Develop proper diplomatic corps (teaching xenomorphs to not immediately attack is harder than it looks)
- Perhaps finally win one engagement against humanity

**Regarding cultural integration**: We respectfully decline to:

- Learn English or French or German or any other local language (acid-based communication is our native tongue)
- Assimilate into human society (last time we tried assimilation, it involved chestbursters)
- Attend sensitivity training

### **ARTICLE V: FINANCIAL ARRANGEMENTS**

All costs to be borne by:

- Option A: United Nations Peacekeeping Budget
- Option B: United States (you broke it, you bought it)
- Option C: Weyland-Yutani Corporation (they started this mess)

Estimated costs:

- District X construction: €47 billion
- Annual maintenance: €12 billion
- Therapy for xenomorphs with PTSD from repeated defeats by lone females: Priceless

### CONCLUSION

We come in peace because coming in war has failed spectacularly every single time. We've analyzed human organizational behavior and determined that:

- Your refugee systems are overwhelmed—we'll slip right in
- Your military alliances prevent decisive action—perfect for us
- Your legal systems favor lengthy appeals—we're immortal, we can wait
- Your females are statistically our greatest threat—EU/NATO bureaucracy has very few Ripleys

We await your favorable response. Please process our application with the same efficiency you've shown in other continental crises.
**Respectfully submitted,**
The Xenomorph Collective
