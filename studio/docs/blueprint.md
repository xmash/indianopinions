# **App Name**: Young Indians

## Core Features:

- Masthead & Home Feed: A sophisticated newspaper layout featuring a classic masthead, a date line, a featured lead story, and organized sections for policy, culture, and industry.
- Long-Form Editorial Engine: A specialized MDX-powered reading experience with optimized typography, pull quotes, and inline related articles designed for immersive deep dives.
- Article Digest Tool: An AI-powered tool that uses reasoning to summarize long-form essays and reports into actionable 60-second intelligence briefings for busy professionals.
- Category Intelligence Hubs: Dedicated thematic pages for topics like India Tomorrow and Global Indians with mission-driven analysis grids and contributor bio integration.
- Data Lab Dashboard: Visual storytelling module using data cards and chart components to track Indian economic, educational, and infrastructure datasets.
- The Weekly Letter Subscription: Dedicated newsletter onboarding and preview flow designed to cultivate a community of dedicated civic-minded readers.
- Contribution Workflow: An editorial submission system for regional correspondents, analysts, and students to pitch essays and profiles.

## Style Guidelines:

- The palette features a warm Ivory (#F7F3EC) for main pages and Stone (#E6E0D8) for secondary surfaces. Headlines use Charcoal (#343131), while body text is Slate (#4A4747). Editorial Red (#AC0000) and Oxblood (#5D0000) provide scholarly emphasis, with Gold Accent (#9A7A43) for refined highlights.
- A refined pairing featuring Literata (Serif) for headlines to evoke vintage literary authority, and Inter (Sans-serif) for body text and interface elements to ensure objective modern readability.
- Traditional newspaper columns with wide editorial gutters and clear thin-line dividers to distinguish between analysis and news.
- Minimalist Lucide icons using ultra-thin strokes to maintain a quiet, intelligence-focused aesthetic.
- Discrete fade-ins and smooth section transitions that mirror the calm experience of turning a page, avoiding flashy motion.
